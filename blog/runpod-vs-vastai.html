When to Use Each

Choose RunPod When:

  • Serverless inference: RunPod's serverless offering is best-in-class for deploying models as APIs
  • Enterprise reliability: Secure cloud (datacenter-grade) with DDoS protection and network volumes
  • Ease of use: Better UX, more templates, built-in Jupyter — ideal for teams less comfortable with DevOps
  • Production workloads: When uptime and managed infrastructure matter more than raw price

Choose Vast.ai When:

  • Budget is king: Consistently 20-50% cheaper across all GPU types
  • Training workloads: Long-running training jobs where the price savings compound significantly
  • GPU variety: Largest marketplace with more GPU availability and configuration options
  • Spot/interruptible work: Great for experimentation and research where interruptions are acceptable

The Verdict

Vast.ai wins on price (20-50% cheaper), while RunPod wins on features and reliability (serverless, secure cloud, better UX). For training on a budget, choose Vast.ai. For production inference and teams that value managed infrastructure, choose RunPod. Many teams use both — Vast.ai for training, RunPod for serving.
